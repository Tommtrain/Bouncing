Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01 Apr 2025

1. What We Collect

  • OAuth tokens / App IDs: required for authenticating with TikTok.
  • Basic usage logs: non-identifiable diagnostics (e.g., timestamps, request status).

2. How We Use Data

Collected information is used only to:

  1. Authenticate your account with TikTok.
  2. Generate and upload videos you request.
  3. Maintain service stability and troubleshoot errors.
We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Storage & Retention

OAuth tokens are encrypted in transit and at rest. Usage logs are automatically purged within 24 hours. If you revoke access on TikTok, related tokens are deleted instantly.

4. Cookies

The App sets no tracking cookies. TikTok may set its own essential cookies during the OAuth flow; these are governed by TikTok’s policies.

5. Your Rights

Under the GDPR and other applicable laws, you may request access to or deletion of your personal data by emailing dpo@bouncing.com.

6. Security

We follow industry-standard measures (TLS, encryption-at-rest, least-privilege access) to protect your information. No method of transmission is 100 % secure, but we strive to keep risk minimal.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced via the App interface or email. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact dpo@bouncing.com.
